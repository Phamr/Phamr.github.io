Last Major Lab

Due
Wednesday by 11:59pm
Points
100
Submitting
a file upload

For this major lab, you will re-create a website of your choice. You may choose any website (within reason, no inappropriate or overly simple sites) as long as you meet these requirements:

1. You must use an external stylesheet.

2. You should have at least 1 instance of grid or flexbox on your site. Not one of each. Just one of either.

3. Your complete website should be hosted on github.

That's all there is to it. You need to complete your website by this wednesday.

Grading:

Your website will earn a rating of 1-10 based on how similar your website is to the original.

If you meet all three of the requirements above, your rating * 10 will be your grade. Ex: 9/10=>90pts.

You may work with a partner. Only one submission is required.
